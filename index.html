01. IF I'M COMPLETELY NEW TO TRADING, WILL I UNDERSTAND THE MATERIAL?

TRADeg Learning App advocates that it is easier to teach someone from scratch rather than to retrain. Retraining is a big challenge, removing old habits is very difficult. Yet not impossible.
The program contains a separate chapter for beginners. Where all most commonly asked questions are answered - what is a candle, a trend, a flag, a channel, a short, a bid, an ask. As well as - who are the sellers and buyers, how to choose a broker, in which market to start, and much, much more.
According to the experiences of our students, even those whose level before training in the program was equal to zero - achieved the desired results. TRADeg Learning App provides a lot of simple training solutions for beginners; therefore, anyone can succeed in the financial markets. All it takes is persistence and proper mentorship.

02. WILL I BE ABLE TO COMMUNICATE WITH TRADEG LEARNING EDUCATORS AND ASK THEM QUESTIONS PERSONALLY?

Certainly. The Educator Support option includes one on one sessions with Educators. During those sessions, TRADeg Learning Educators will answer all your questions, offer feedback on your trades, and will review your trading algorithm.
This is a very serious program; it is not a movie that you can watch and forget.
You will have to work hard, complete tasks, analyze errors, write your trading algorithm again and again. That will enable you to have a fighting chance at the financial markets and get a taste for financial freedom.

TRADeg Learning App stands by its program and guarantees results, but results have to be earned, and you will have to work hard. After almost every Live session, you will have homework that you should complete.
Each assignment was created personally by TRADeg Learning educators is a necessary stage for a comprehensive understanding of the markets, and trading in general.

Of course. The program is designed in such a way that a fifth-grader will be able to understand the material. In the learning process, you will receive step-by-step instructions and simple tools that you can immediately apply in your trading.
This does not mean that everything will be simple. But with great desire and a responsible approach to learning, you will succeed.

The trading strategy taught by TRADeg Learning Educators works within absolutely any market and does not depend on any particular timeframe. Selecting a market and/or a timeframe comes down to personal preference.

Therefore, it does not really matter which market you trade or on which timeframe you use. You will receive a whole set of rules and tools for safe trading in any market.
If you have not decided which market to trade or want to change - problem. TRADeg Learning Educators will tell you the pros and cons of trading in vast number of markets, and you will choose the one that is right for you.
